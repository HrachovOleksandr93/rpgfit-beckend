# RPG Professions — Lineage 2 Style Class Progression

> Three-tier class transfer system inspired by Lineage 2.
> Tier 1 = starting class, Tier 2 = first class transfer, Tier 3 = second class transfer (master class).
>
> Stats: **STR** (Strength), **DEX** (Dexterity), **CON** (Constitution)

---

## 1. Combat

**Category slug:** `combat`
**Activities:** Boxing, martial arts, wrestling, kickboxing, fencing
**Theme:** Close-quarters fighters, gladiators, martial masters

> Those who walk the path of Combat embrace the fury of close-quarters battle. From dusty sparring pits to the grand arenas, these warriors forge their bodies into living weapons. Only the most relentless survive the climb to become legends of the fighting pit.

| Tier | Name | Description | Primary Stat | Secondary Stat |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Fighter** | A raw recruit who has taken their first steps into hand-to-hand combat. Every bruise is a lesson, every sparring round a step toward mastery. | STR | CON |
| 2 | **Gladiator** | A seasoned arena combatant who has survived countless bouts and earned the crowd's respect. Their strikes carry the weight of hard-won experience and disciplined technique. | STR | CON |
| 3 | **Titan Breaker** | An unstoppable force of martial devastation whose fists shatter defenses like siege engines. Legends say the ground trembles when a Titan Breaker enters the arena. | STR | CON |
